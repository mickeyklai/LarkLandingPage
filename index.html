The Novel

Independent

She built her life on discipline.

He built his on getting exactly what he wants.

When his attention lands on her, everything becomes a test: of control, of desire, and of the loyalties she thought were unbreakable. He doesn't chase; he acquires. And he has never lost anything he decided was his.

The most dangerous part isn't his obsession.

It's the way she's starting to respond.

Independent is a dark romance. It doesn't promise a safe journey, but it promises an unforgettable one.

The Reader

This book is for you if…

  • You love a hero who takes what he wants, and you're rooting for him anyway
  • You read for the tension as much as the resolution
  • Morally complex characters feel more interesting to you than safe ones
  • Obsession, possessiveness, and power dynamics are your comfort zone
  • You've dog-eared half of every dark romance you've ever owned and felt zero guilt about it
  • You want a story that earns its ending instead of just handing one to you

The Author

Lark Elwood

Lark Elwood writes dark romance for readers who aren't afraid of the complicated parts: the power plays, the obsession, the characters who make choices you wouldn't but understand completely.

Independent is her debut novel. She believes every great dark romance has to earn its ending, and she spent a long time making sure this one does.

She writes for the readers who finish a book at 2am and lie awake thinking about it. This one is for you.
