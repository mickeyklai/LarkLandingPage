Information collection

This website (larkelwood.com) is operated by Lark Elwood. When you visit or use the site, certain information may be collected automatically, and additional information may be collected when you choose to interact with forms or features.

Usage and device data. Like most websites, servers and analytics tools may process technical information such as your IP address, general location (e.g. region or city), browser type, device type, pages viewed, and approximate time spent on the site. This helps measure traffic, improve performance, and understand how readers discover and use the site.

Meta (Facebook) Pixel. This site may use the Meta Pixel, a tool provided by Meta Platforms, Inc., to measure advertising effectiveness and to deliver or optimize relevant ads. The Pixel may collect or receive information about your visit, including through cookies and similar technologies, and may associate it with your Meta account when applicable. How Meta uses this information is described in Meta’s policies. You can learn more in Meta’s Privacy Policy and adjust some ad preferences through your Meta account settings.

TikTok Pixel. This site may use the TikTok Pixel to measure visits and conversions from TikTok campaigns and to improve ad delivery. TikTok may collect usage data via cookies and similar technologies. Details are covered in TikTok’s Privacy Policy.

Email and forms. If you join a mailing list, apply for an advance reader copy, or submit information through an embedded form (for example, forms hosted by a third-party service such as Tally), the details you provide are processed by that service under its own terms and privacy policy. Only submit information you are comfortable sharing.

Cookies

This site uses cookies and similar storage technologies placed by Lark Elwood and by third-party partners (including Meta and TikTok) to support analytics, advertising measurement, and site functionality. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help recognize browsers or remember preferences.

You can control cookies through your browser settings by blocking or deleting cookies. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit some features or cause parts of the site to behave differently. Third-party opt out tools (where available) may also help limit interest-based advertising across platforms.

Data security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect this site and the information processed through it. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Data may be processed in the United States or other countries where service providers operate, which may have different data protection rules than your place of residence.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, privacy laws may give you rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information, or to opt out of sale or sharing for targeted advertising. Laws vary by region.

To exercise rights that may apply to you, you can contact the operator of third-party tools you use (such as Meta or TikTok) through their account or privacy portals, and adjust cookie or ad settings in your browser or device. For questions specific to this site, you may reach out via the contact options listed on the Follow section of the homepage.

This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when revisions are published.
